### Dataset & EDA — Week 9 Interim

- **Source:** Synthetic Hebrew military-slang utterances generated by `prompt_factory1240`
  across 4 platforms: whatsapp, tweet, reddit, diary.
- **Size:** 100 raw → 85 after G-Eval LLM-as-a-Judge filter (85.0% kept).
- **Label space:** 8 PTSD-symptom labels, multi-label
  (mean cardinality 1.05; includes hard-negative class with zero labels).
- **Class balance:** rarest = `avoidance` (6 examples),
  most frequent = `sleep_disturbance` (25 examples).
  JS-divergence train↔test = 0.0039.
- **Split:** Iterative Stratification (Sechidis et al., 2011) 75/25,
  seed=1240. Every label present in both folds (all sanity gates passed).
  Train N=76 | Test N=24.
- **Stratifier:** `iterative_train_test_split` (0.2.0).
- **Known limitations:** Small N, fully synthetic origin, no human inter-annotator
  agreement measured yet (planned Week 10).
